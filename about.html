Pinnacle was born on 1st November 2001 with a modest strength of just 8 students, offering just one diploma course in 800sft office space, by a wife and husband Y.Sridevi and Y.Madhu Sudhan who are first generation entrepreneurs. They started with the endeavor to create True Hospitality Professionals rather than students with mere paper qualifications. It was with this strong commitment, that the college under Prabhath education society.Today Pinnacle is a name to reckon with in the field of hotel management education the group has 70000sft campuses spread across two campuses including a student hostel. In the year 2012 Pinnacle has taken over Green Fields College of catering and hotel management which is also affiliated with Osmania University and the Board of intermediate education.
