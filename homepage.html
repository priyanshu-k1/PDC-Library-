BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on the application of computer science. It is a popular course among students who are interested in a career in the IT industry.

BSC

Bachelor of Science in Clinical Nutrition (B.Sc. Clinical Nutrition) is a three-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on the science of nutrition and its application in the prevention and treatment of disease.

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate degree program that provides students with a broad foundation in business knowledge and skills. The BBA curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including: Accounting,Economics,Finance,etc

BCOM

Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) is a three-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of commerce, which is the exchange of goods and services.
